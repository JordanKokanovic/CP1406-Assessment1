What is Cosplay?

Cosplay is a relatively new subculture in which paricipants wear costumes and dress up as a specific character. Cosplayers tend to dress up as characters from their favourite manga, anime, comic books, television shows and movies. Cosplay is now a favourite hobby around the world with both local and international competitions as well as other forms of media centered on Cosplay activities.
